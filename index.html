Benefits of Being a Developer

No Degree Required!

No degree? No problem! If you are wondering if you need a degree to get into the web development field the answer is NO! All it takes is hard work and dedication! Skill is what matters in this industry.

High Average Salary!

So no degree? What about the pay? The answer is quite surprising. The average pay for web developers is $76,000 per year according to indeed. This can vary depending on location and level of expertise. A high Salary is just one of the many benefits of becoming a developer.

Limitless Potential

Companies like Amazon, Facebook, Google and Instagram (to name a few) became successful by achieving success on the internet. You too can make your dreams a reality and make your own business or web application that changes millions of people's lives for generations to come!
